Step 6 of 6

Monitoring Overview

You're live. Here's what happens next — and a few free tools to watch your score on the way up.

What happens next
1
Letters draft within hours
Round 1 dispute letters draft for each bureau using the items you flagged. Operator reviews + queues for filing.
2
Bureaus respond within 30 days
By federal law (FCRA §1681i), each bureau has 30 days to respond. We'll notify you the moment a response lands.
3
Verification codes by SMS
Bureaus may text verification codes to your phone. Forward them to verify@memelli.com or paste in your dashboard.
4
Round 2 plans automatically
Whatever doesn't clear in Round 1, we re-strategize for Round 2 with stronger reasons (MOV, validation, CFPB). Up to 4 rounds covered.
